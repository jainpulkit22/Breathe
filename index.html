Hospital

Our objective is to connect all the registered hospitals through our portal so that whenever there is a need of an organ at any hospital , they can directly check the availability for the same on our portal and get them arranged if the compatibility of the patient matches and in a similar way whenever any organ is available at any hospital we shall notify to all the other hospitals and the recipients who are in need of the same with the time for which the organ is available.

Donor

We aim to provide a centralised system for storing data about donors and those in need of organs, while maintaining anonymity between them. Recipients will be notified in case an organ is available nearby in a hospital registered on our platform. The notification in the form of a text/email and will be redirected only to the filtered users who are in need of the specified organ.Our services will help donors in pledging their organs and saving lives. Spreading awareness about organ transplants and related misconceptions are one of our main concerns. Organ donors can help us in this noble cause and save invaluable lives.

Recipient

Recipients will recieve a notification in case the organ they require is available in their vicinity. Recipients will need to provide proper documents and prescription from certified doctors. If the recipient meets the basic requirements they will be put in a list and will be notified whenever an organ matches their requirement.

Facts you need to know!

Every year nearly 5 lakh people die just due to the unavilability of organs.

Nearly 2.2 lakh people await kidney transplant , of which around only 15,000 end up receiving a kidney.

At a given point of time, just in India about 1 lakh people anticipate and only 1,000 get liver transplant .

Even a cadaver or deceased donor can save up to 8 lives, but despite this organ donation is a neglected issue .

Lack of knowledge, awareness, infrastructure are some of the reasons behind shortage of organ donation in India .
